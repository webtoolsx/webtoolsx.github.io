How to Use

  1. Step 1: Enter Your Date of Birth
    Select your birth date in the "Date of Birth" field.
  2. Step 2: Enter the Current Date
    By default, today's date is selected. You can change it if needed.
  3. Step 3: Click "Calculate"
    Press the "Calculate" button to see your age in years, months, and days.
  4. Step 4: View Results
    Your exact age will be displayed instantly.
  5. Step 5: Reset (Optional)
    Click "Reset" to clear the fields and start again.
Note: This tool calculates your age based on the dates you provide.

Formula Explanation:

  1. Step 1: Subtract Birth Date from Current Date
    The difference gives your age in years, months, and days.
  2. Step 2: Handle Month and Day Calculation
    If the current day/month is less than birth day/month, adjust accordingly.
  3. Step 3: Example Calculation
    If you were born on 2000-05-15 and today is 2024-06-10:
    
Years: 24 (2024-2000)
Months: 0 (June-May)
Days: 26 (10-15, adjusted for previous month)
