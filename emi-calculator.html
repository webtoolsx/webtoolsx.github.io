Formula Explanation

  1. EMI is calculated using the formula: 
    EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N-1]
    Where:
    • P: Principal Loan Amount
    • R: Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate / 12 / 100)
    • N: Loan Tenure in Months

What is a Loan EMI Calculator?

A Loan EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) Calculator is a tool that helps borrowers estimate their monthly loan payments. It calculates the EMI based on:

  • Loan Amount (P) – The total money borrowed.
  • Interest Rate (r) – The annual rate converted to a monthly rate.
  • Loan Tenure (n) – The total number of months for repayment.

The EMI is calculated using the formula:

EMI = [P × r × (1 + r)n] / [(1 + r)n - 1]

This tool helps users plan their finances by understanding repayment schedules, total interest paid, and affordability before taking a loan. It is commonly used for home, car, and personal loans.
